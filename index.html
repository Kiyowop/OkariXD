Your Boyfriend/Peter


Your Boyfriend (real name Peter), serving as the titular main antagonist in Your Boyfriend and is the game's driving force. He is a recurring and sentient character who develops a possessive and obsessive relationship with Y/N. <3


Appearance

Peter appears to have a gray body. He has darken blue eyes and absolutely no hair. He wears black pants, a dark blue T-shirt with a white and black heart, and a black vest. He also wears black shoes that match his pants. Regardless of your gender, Peter is revealed to be considerably taller than you, Peter's exact height is revealed to be around at 195 cm (~6'5")


Personality

Peter is obsessive, possessive, unstable, and violent. Peter also tends to stalk whoever he loves, which is Y/N themselves. He's a deeply disturbed and dangerous individual who will stop at nothing to gain your affection. Peter is a man whose heart is suffering from his unhealthy love. He is an obsessive, possessive, unstable, tough, and desperate psychopath who will do everything possible to show how much he loves you in his perverted sense.


Abilities


Immense Stamina:
Peter has a great amount of stamina as he can run indefinitely and seems quicker.

He is much physically stronger than he looks, he is able to knock you out by smashing your head against the window of his van, to taking down Don Williams, a taller and physically buffer man, and one artwork Fuboo shows that Peter is able to crack a computer monitor with brute force using just one of his hands.
